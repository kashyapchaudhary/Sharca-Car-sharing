How can we help you?

🚗 Booking a Ride

How do I book a ride?

+
From the Search tab, simply type in your current location (Origin) and where you want to go (Destination). The map will calculate the route and show you available drivers traveling the same way! Click "Book Seat" to send a request to the driver.

Can I cancel my request?

+
Yes! Before the driver accepts your request, you can cancel it freely from your My Trips tab. Once a driver accepts, we ask that you communicate with the driver before cancelling to respect their time.

💸 Payments

When do I pay for my ride?

+
You only pay after the journey is successfully completed! When the driver marks the ride as complete on their app, a "Pay Now" button will appear in your My Trips tab. Clicking it will open our secure checkout page.

What payment methods are supported? UPI & CARD

+
We currently support secure digital payments including UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit Cards, and Debit Cards. You can also select the Cash option if you handed physical money to the driver directly.

🛡️ Safety & Trust

Are the drivers verified?

+
Yes. All drivers are verified through our platform before they can publish a ride. You will see their profile picture, verified checkmark, car model, and license plate number when you book the ride. Always verify the car plate before getting in!

What if I left something in the car?

+
You can find your past trips under the "Completed" section in your My Trips tab. We recommend noting down your driver's contact number before the trip ends. If you can't reach them, contact our support team.
