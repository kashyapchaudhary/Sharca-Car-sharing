How can we help you?

🚗 Getting Started

How do I offer a ride?

+
It's simple! Navigate to the Add Ride tab. Enter your starting location, destination, the date and time of departure, how many empty seats you have, and the price per seat. Click "Publish Ride" and it will immediately be visible to users searching for that route.

How do I accept ride requests?

+
Go to your Dashboard. When a user books a seat on one of your published rides, a request card will appear here. You can review the request and click "Accept" to confirm the booking. You will then see an option to mark the ride as "Completed" once the journey is done.

💸 Earnings & Payments

How is my wallet balance calculated?

+
Your wallet balance reflects only the trips that have been officially marked as PAID in our system. Once you complete a ride and the user submits their payment via the app checkout (or confirms cash payment), the funds (minus the 20% platform fee) are instantly added to your Wallet balance on the Earnings page.

Why does my ride say "Pending Payment"? PENDING

+
If you marked a ride as "Completed" on your dashboard, but the user has not yet completed the checkout process on their phone, the ride will show up in your Earnings history as "Pending Payment". Kindly remind the user to complete the checkout via the app!

What is the Sharca Platform Fee?

+
To maintain the servers, provide 24/7 support, and continue improving the app, Sharca deducts a flat 20% platform fee from the total price paid by the user. You keep 80% of the earnings.

🛡️ Safety & Guidelines

What are the safety rules for drivers?

+
As a verified driver, you must ensure your vehicle is in good condition, hold a valid driver's license, and obey all traffic laws. We strictly prohibit driving under the influence or reckless driving. Ensure your license plate matches your profile details.
